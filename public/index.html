Cyber Hackathon

Welcome to Cyber Hackthon.
Cyber Crime Cell, Central Crime Branch of Greater Chennai Police in Association with SRM Institute of Science & Technology Ramapuram presents Cyber Hackathon. This event comprises two distinct stages. Initially, teams will engage in an online submission phase, presenting solution concepts.
This is a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology.


The shortlisted teams will then advance to the second round. In the subsequent phase, these selected teams will convene on campus to showcase their operational solutions to a panel of experts, with a duration of 2 Days.
The only contraint is the time!



About Alibi:

Alibi is India’s first NABL Accredited Cyber Forensic Private Lab, based in Kerala and with Operational Centres in Tamil Nadu and Delhi, specializing solely in cyber forensics and cyber intelligence. Alibi is a certified ISO/IEC 17001:2017 and ISO/IEC 27001:2013 company.

Training Program:

Alibi offers to provide free training in the field of Mobile and Hard disk forensics to the top 5 teams in the Cyber Hackathon, which costs about Rs.45,000 per person. The training and certification will be immensely beneficial to the trainees’ careers. The training period is 40 hours, and certification will be provided at the completion of the training.

Benefits:

The trainee will be exposed to actual forensic procedural approach for dealing with any crime involving digital evidence. The trainee will also have the chance to work on actual cases and gain hands-on experience with globally renowned licensed Cyber forensic software tools.

Duration and Schedule Details:

The training is available both online and offline formats. Offline training is only available at the Chennai office of Alibi.

Training will take place between the 3rd March and 15th May, 2024.
