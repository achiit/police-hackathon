Cyber HACKATHON

Welcome to Cyber Hackthon.
The CHAT club from the IT department at SRMIST's Ramapuram campus, in partnership with the Chennai City Cyber, is orchestrating the "Cyber Hackathon." This event comprises two distinct stages. Initially, teams will engage in an online submission phase, presenting solution concepts.
This is a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology.


The top 20 teams will then advance to the second round. In the subsequent phase, these selected teams will convene on campus to showcase their operational solutions to a panel of experts, with a duration of 2 Days.
The only contraint is the time!
